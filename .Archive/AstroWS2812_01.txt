 * Sun Position LED Ring Display
 * Uses a 332 LED ring to show the progression of the sun throughout the day
 * 
 * Features:
 * - Fetches real sunrise/sunset times from online API
 * - Maps 24 hours to 332 LEDs (approx 4.3 minutes per LED)
 * - Shows orange gradient from sunrise position to current time
 * - Yellow LED marks current time position (when during daylight)
 * - All LEDs off during night hours (sunset to sunrise)
 * - Updates times hourly to maintain accuracy
